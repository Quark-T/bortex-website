Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can move from Free to Premium at any time. Changes are immediate and billing starts on the day of the upgrade.

What happens if I exceed the Free plan limits?

You will receive a notification when you are close to the limit. You can upgrade to Premium to continue without interruptions.

Is the Free plan really free?

Yes. The Free plan is completely free forever. It includes essential chat and 1 user, perfect for getting started without complexity.

Can I cancel at any time?

Absolutely. There are no contractual constraints. Cancel whenever you want directly from the dashboard, without contacting support.
