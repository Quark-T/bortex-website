Bortex chat widget for your website

A chat widget is more than a button on your site. With Bortex it becomes a complete system: automatic AI replies, live conversations, video calls to visitors, Shopify integration and custom invite bubbles.

Complete guide to installing, configuring and getting the most out of the Bortex widget: from copying the JS snippet to managing voice and video calls with your visitors.

Do you have a website and want your visitors to contact you in real time, receive automatic AI replies, or even start a video call directly from your site? The Bortex chat widget does all of this — and it installs in under five minutes with a single line of code.

What is the Bortex chat widget

The Bortex widget is an interactive chat button you can add to any website: a showcase site, e-commerce store, landing page, portfolio or corporate portal. Once installed, it appears in the corner of your site and lets visitors open a real-time chat window.

Behind the widget is the entire Bortex infrastructure: the AI replies automatically when you are unavailable, you can jump in live from the dashboard, and visitors can also request a voice or video call directly from the chat. All in one solution, with no additional plugins to install.

How to install the widget on your website

Installation is designed to be fast even for non-developers. From the Bortex dashboard, in the Widget section, you will find your personalised JavaScript snippet. Simply copy and paste it before the </body> tag of every page on your site:

<script src="https://www.bortex.site/loader.js?apikey=YOUR_API_KEY" defer></script>

If you use a CMS such as WordPress, Webflow or Shopify, you can paste the snippet into the "Custom Code" area or through a plugin that injects code into the header or footer. With Shopify the integration is even more direct: Bortex offers a native app that installs the widget automatically on your store without touching any code.

Compatibility

The Bortex widget works on any HTML site, WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace and Shopify. It requires no external libraries, does not slow down page load time and adapts automatically to desktop and mobile.

Customise the widget to match your brand

From the Bortex dashboard you can modify every visual aspect of the widget: the button colour, the icon, the assistant avatar, the display name shown in the chat and the position on screen (bottom-right corner, left or centre). The chat opens with a personalised welcome message and can show suggested frequently asked questions you have already configured.

A particularly effective feature is the invite bubble: a small text speech bubble that appears next to the widget a few seconds after the visitor arrives. You can set the text ("Hi, can I help you?"), the delay before it appears, the duration it stays visible and the colour. This kind of proactive interaction significantly increases the chat open rate.

Live chat: reply to visitors in real time

When a visitor opens the chat and types a message, you receive a real-time notification on the Bortex dashboard. From there you can reply directly, see the visitor profile (the page they are on, operating system, language) and manage multiple conversations simultaneously in an organised way.

If you are not online, the Bortex AI assistant replies automatically in your place, maintaining the tone and information you configured during setup. This means your site never stops being "present" for visitors, even outside business hours.

AI replies on your behalf: configuration and limits

The Bortex AI assistant is trained on the information you provide: your products, services, opening hours, return policies and FAQs. Every automatic reply reflects your brand voice and can be refined over time using feedback from real conversations.

The free plan includes 100 AI messages per month: enough to get started and test how it works. With the Premium plan, messages become unlimited, with routing to advanced cloud providers for faster and more accurate replies.

Bortex is not just a widget: it is an assistant that works with you

The idea behind Bortex goes well beyond a website chat tool. Bortex is built as an assistant that helps manage day-to-day work: organising commitments, supporting tasks, bringing structure to schedules and giving continuity to events and priorities. The widget is the public-facing extension of that same operational intelligence.

That means the same logic Bortex uses to help organise your work can also be applied outward: replying to website visitors, welcoming requests, guiding users and customers, and turning traffic into useful conversations. It is not a separate add-on. It is your assistant becoming visible online as well.

Ready to take the business step?

If you already use Bortex as a personal assistant, you can turn it into a business tool by activating your own widget on your website. That way Bortex does not just help with agenda, tasks and events, it also becomes your digital presence for customers and visitors.

The transition is natural: from an assistant that organises your work, to an assistant that also communicates, replies and converts on the web.

Shopify integration: the widget that sells

If you run an e-commerce store on Shopify, Bortex integrates natively with your shop. The widget can answer questions about products, shipping, returns and order status — directly in the chat, without customers having to navigate between pages or wait for an email.

The Shopify integration also lets you connect the AI to your catalogue data, making replies contextual and precise. The result is an assistant that knows your store like an expert salesperson, available 24 hours a day.

Video calls and phone contact with visitors

One of Bortex's most powerful and distinctive features is the ability to start a video call directly from the chat. When a visitor needs more personal support — for example during a high-value purchase, a consultation or the resolution of a complex problem — they can request to speak with you on video in just a few seconds.

You receive a notification on the dashboard, accept the call and within seconds you are face to face with your customer directly in the browser, with nothing to install. This level of direct contact transforms the chat into a top-tier sales and support tool.

The free plan includes 60 minutes of video calls per month. With the Premium plan, video calls are unlimited — ideal for agencies, consultants, high average order value online stores and anyone who wants to offer truly personalised support.

Why video calls change conversion rates

Direct contact — voice and face — breaks down the psychological barriers of online distrust. Customers who speak directly with a representative before making a purchase convert at significantly higher rates than those who interact only via text chat or email. Bortex brings this dynamic directly to your website.

Push notifications: stay in touch after the visit

Bortex also integrates a web push notification system: you can ask visitors for permission to send notifications and then reach them directly in the browser — even when they are not on your site — with updates, offers or personalised messages.

The free plan allows up to 4 push campaigns per month. With Premium, campaigns are unlimited, with advanced segmentation tools and scheduled delivery.
