About

We are building AI that is useful, practical and human

BORTEX was built to simplify everyday work for people and teams: smarter conversations, practical automation and better execution in one experience.

Our Vision

Turn the AI assistant into a daily working tool

We believe an assistant should not just sound intelligent. It should understand context, suggest actions and help users move from idea to execution.

That is why BORTEX combines conversation, personal organization, task automation and business integrations in one ecosystem that scales from personal use to professional use.

The Pillars of BORTEX

The principles behind product, technology and roadmap decisions

Natural Conversation

A conversational experience that is clear, fast and built around results, ready to be used every day.

Operational Automation

From reminders to more complex tasks: fewer clicks, less friction, more execution.

Intelligent Organization

Calendar, notifications and priorities in one flow, with AI support that adapts to your pace.

Scale from Personal to Business

Same platform, new levels: start with personal use cases and expand with integrations like Shopify.

Product Direction

One coherent ecosystem across web, desktop and mobile

Central Chat

The `bortex.html` page is the operational center: conversation, support, decisions and real-time task flow.

Desktop & iOS Experience

Continuity across devices so you can keep context and productivity wherever you work.

Useful Integrations

API, Shopify, WordPress and advanced modules to connect BORTEX to your actual stack.
