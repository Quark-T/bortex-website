Remote PC control with Bortex

With the Bortex ecosystem you can take control of your computer from anywhere: from your smartphone via the iOS app, from the web dashboard, or from the Electron desktop app. An AI assistant that never leaves you without access to what you need.

📱 iOS App 🖥️ Electron Desktop 🌐 Web Platform 🤖 AI Assistant
March 30, 2026 · 7 min read · Product

How many times have you been away from home and needed to do something on your computer? Launch a file, check a status, move the mouse during a presentation? With Bortex all of this becomes simple: your AI assistant is the bridge between you and your PC, wherever you are.

Why remote control changes the way we work

Modern work no longer happens only at a desk. We are in meetings, travelling, in another office, and often our computer is on but unreachable. Traditional remote control tools solve the problem in a technical way, but they remain rigid, hard to use, and lack intelligence.

Bortex approaches the problem differently: it does not just give you a screen to look at from a distance, but gives you a connected ecosystem where the AI assistant understands what you want to do and helps you do it — from whichever device you have in your hand at that moment.

The Bortex ecosystem: three layers, one unified experience

Remote control in Bortex does not depend on a single component. It works thanks to the collaboration of three technology layers that work together transparently.

📱 iOS App — The intelligent remote always in your pocket

The Bortex iPhone app includes a mirroring and control screen that turns your phone into a wireless trackpad and keyboard for your PC. You can move the cursor with your finger, click, pinch-to-zoom, type text and send commands in real time. No complicated setup required: open the app, connect to your device and you are already operational.

🖥️ Electron App — The local agent that listens and responds

On your computer, Bortex runs through an Electron-based desktop application. This app acts as a local agent: it stays listening, receives commands from the iOS app or from the web platform, and executes them directly on the system. It can control open windows, manage the computer's state, interact with active applications and respond to AI assistant commands without you ever touching the physical keyboard.

🌐 Web Platform — The command center that coordinates everything

The Bortex web dashboard is the heart of the ecosystem. From here you can monitor the status of your PC, send commands, chat with the AI assistant to delegate operations, view activity logs and configure automations. You do not need to install anything on the device you are accessing from: just a browser is enough.

The real competitive advantage

Unlike classic remote desktop tools, Bortex does not just show you a screen: it gives you an assistant that understands context, executes actions for you and adapts to the device you are using. This is the quality leap that makes the difference between a tool and a true operational assistant.

What you can concretely do with Bortex remote control

The Bortex remote control system is not designed only for IT professionals or technical users. It is built for anyone who wants to stay productive even when away from their workstation. Here are some real-world scenarios:

  • You are in a meeting and need to open a document on the office PC: you do it in thirty seconds from your smartphone without interrupting anyone.
  • You are giving a presentation in a conference room: you use the iOS app as a remote control to advance slides and move the pointer.
  • You are away from home and your computer is running a process: with the web platform you check the status and, if necessary, stop it or manage the output.
  • You want to delegate an action to the AI assistant: you write in chat what to do, and Bortex executes it on your PC without you needing to be physically present.
  • You need to send text to the computer from your phone: you use the iOS app's virtual keyboard to type directly into the active window on the desktop.

Security and control: your privacy first

Granting remote access to your computer is an important decision. Bortex is designed with security as a priority: the connection between devices is encrypted, access is authenticated through your Bortex account, and you can always view and revoke active sessions from the dashboard.

You are not using a generic third-party service: you are using your own infrastructure, with your own assistant, within your own ecosystem. This means fewer external dependencies and more control over what happens.

The AI assistant that acts for you, not just responds

The most powerful aspect of Bortex remote control is not the trackpad or screen streaming. It is the AI assistant behind it. Instead of having to manually navigate through windows and applications, you can simply tell Bortex what you want to achieve, and it will find a way to do it.

This is the difference between a passive tool and an active agent. Bortex does not wait for you to do everything yourself: it understands context, suggests actions, executes tasks and alerts you when something requires your attention. Remote control thus becomes not an extension of the keyboard, but an extension of your operational capability.

In summary: why choose Bortex for remote control

✓ A unified ecosystem connecting iOS, Electron desktop and web platform without complex configuration
✓ Integrated AI assistant that executes actions for you, not just suggests them
✓ Wireless trackpad and keyboard via iPhone for precise and natural control
✓ Web dashboard accessible from any browser, no additional installation required
✓ Secure authenticated connections with full control over active sessions

The future: an assistant that takes initiative

The Bortex roadmap includes a further evolutionary leap in remote control: proactive automations, intelligent notifications when something on your PC needs attention, scheduled task execution and integration with other devices in the ecosystem. Remote control will no longer be a feature used in emergencies, but an everyday operational mode — natural and powerful. With Bortex, your PC finally becomes your 360-degree operational assistant — even when you are not in front of the screen.
