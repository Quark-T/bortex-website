Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. What this policy covers

This Cookie Policy explains which cookies and similar technologies BORTEX may use on the public website and related product experiences, which purposes they serve and how visitors can control them.

2. Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: needed for security, session continuity and core website functionality.
  • Preference cookies: used to remember interface choices or non-critical settings.
  • Analytics cookies: used to understand website usage and improve content, features and performance.
  • Third-party cookies: may be set by integrated tools or service providers where relevant.

3. Why cookies are used

Cookies may be used to keep the website secure, preserve session state, analyse product interest and improve user experience. BORTEX does not rely on cookie-based tracking beyond what is necessary for functionality, consented analytics or clearly disclosed integrations.

Where consent is legally required, non-essential cookies should only be activated after the user has granted permission through the relevant consent flow.

4. Managing your preferences

Users can control cookie preferences through browser settings and, where available, through consent tools shown on the website.

  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
