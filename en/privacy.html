Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

BORTEX, developed and managed by Quark Digital Agency, is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and their customers. This Privacy Policy describes in detail how we collect, use, store and protect personal data in compliance with EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

2. Data Controller

  • Company Name: Quark Digital Agency
  • VAT Number: 15448511004

3. Data Collected

We collect various types of data to provide and improve our Service:

A. User Data (Customer)

  • Registration Data: First Name, Last Name, Email Address, Password (encrypted).
  • Billing Data: Address, VAT/Tax Code (managed via Stripe).

B. Visitor Data (End Users)

  • Navigation Data: IP Address (anonymised where possible), User Agent, Referring Page (Referrer).
  • Conversation Data: Messages exchanged via Live Chat (text, any attachments).

4. Purposes of Processing

  • Service Delivery: To enable the operation of the Live Chat and AI Assistant.
  • Account and Payment Management: To manage user accounts and process subscription payments.
  • Service Communications: To send account notifications, technical updates or changes to terms.
  • Security and Defence: To prevent fraud, abuse and defend the Controller's rights in legal proceedings.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of a Contract: For the delivery of the Service requested by the user.
  • Compliance with Legal Obligations: For invoicing and retention of tax data.
  • Legitimate Interest: For platform security and service improvement.
  • Consent: For optional marketing activities.

6. Data Recipients

Personal data may be shared with third-party providers of essential services (Sub-processors), bound by confidentiality agreements:

  • Stripe: For payment processing.
  • Hetzner / VPS Provider: For infrastructure hosting (Servers located in the EU).

Data is NOT sold or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

7. Data Transfers Outside the EU

The platform and databases reside on servers located within the European Union. No systematic transfer of data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) is envisaged.

8. Retention Period

  • Account Data: Retained until the account is deleted by the user.
  • Logs and Chat: Retained for the time strictly necessary for the service purposes or as required by law (e.g. 10 years for tax data).

9. Data Subject Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request rectification or erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, send a request to: info@bortex.site
